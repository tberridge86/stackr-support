Reporting a Problem

To help us investigate quickly, please include:

  • Your iPhone model and iOS version
  • The screen or feature you were using
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • A screenshot or screen recording, where possible

Beta Testing

Stackr is currently in beta. Card scan recognition, market information and app features may continue to improve during testing.

For Market scanning feedback, tell us which physical card you scanned and whether the returned card, set and card number were correct.
